Working

by Robert Caro

Notes

January 14, 2026

I heard that I must read Robert Caro's multi-volume biograpgy of Lyndon Johnson. These books are exactly the opposite of the kind I typically read (despite still being nonfiction; it's going to be very diffficult get me to break from that habit even once), so I went ahead and ordered the first volume. When ordering it, ThriftBooks1 suggested also a shorter book by Caro, titled Working. I figured that—if a guy can write so well about people like LBJ and Robert Moses, so well that even I was compelled to read their works—I could gain a lot from understanding explicitly what sets them apart in their craft.

I am working my way through the book, and I'll write down my thoughts here when I finish in a few days.
